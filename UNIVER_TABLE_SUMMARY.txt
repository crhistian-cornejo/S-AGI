================

FORMULAS INCLUDED
=================

CELL FORMULAS (Rows 1-10):
  F1:F10 = =D{row}*E{row}

  Example:
  F1 = =D1*E1 → 5 × $1,299.99 = $6,499.95
  F2 = =D2*E2 → 8 × $399.50 = $3,196.00

SUMMARY FORMULAS (Row 11):
  D11 = =SUM(D1:D10) → Total quantity = 96
  F11 = =SUM(F1:F10) → Grand total = $13,289.89

NUMBER FORMATS:
  Currency Columns:     $#,##0.00
  Quantity Columns:     0
  Date Columns:         yyyy-mm-dd
